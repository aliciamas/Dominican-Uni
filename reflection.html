Reflecting on the Process of Creating my e-Portfolio

As I reflect on my e-Portfolio assignment, I am both surprised and proud of the work I have created over the years. The process of compiling my work, projects, and assignments into a single, organized, digital website collection has provided me with the opportunity to look back on my growth throughout the course of the Library and Information Science (LIS) program. It is clear to me now how the pieces of my academic journey come together to support the five learning goals and their associated outcomes for the program. While the task of compiling this portfolio required significant time and effort, it was well worth the investment as it allowed me to see my work through a new lens. My e-Portfolio not only showcases my work but also highlights the diverse areas of librarianship I have engaged with and the skills I have developed.
