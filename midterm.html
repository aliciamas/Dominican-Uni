Friends of Palo Alto Library's Monthly Sale

With over 50,000 used books, records, videos, and magazines at our monthly sale, we'd like to give a special thank you to our generous donors, including the Palo Alto Library.

The Main Room

Location: Along the North Driveway, adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center. The Main Room features hardbound books starting at $1 each and paperbacks at 50 cents each. You can also bring your large book donations to this room.

General Main Room Guidelines: Due to space limitations and health and safety concerns, we can only allow 160 customers in the Main Room at a time. On Saturdays, tickets will be distributed from 8 to 11 AM. Entry will be granted in the order of your numbered ticket. Further, you may take a maximum of 12 books off the shelves at a time.

Shopping Guidelines: After selecting your books, please purchase them and exit through the east door. There you will join the end of the line at the north door to reenter in the order of the remaining tickets. The 12-book limit will remain in effect past noon if there’s still a line outside.

The New Room

Location: Near Cubberley Track Pavilion and Greendell School. Explore our collection of children's books, music, videos, and toys in the New Room. Most items are priced between $0.50 and $1. Here you'll find: Picture books; Children’s fiction and nonfiction; CDs and DVDs; and Resources for parents and teachers. Saturday sale reminder: During the first hour, please select no more than 12 books at a time. Bring them to the cashiers to hold until you’re ready to check out.

Bargain Books Room

Location: Rooms K6 and K7. Special Sunday Offer: All prices in the bargain books room are half off! Extra Savings: Purchase a grocery bag for $5 and fill it with as many books as you can fit! Please note: The Children's and Bargain Books Rooms open one hour earlier than the Main Room on Saturday.

Gift Certificates

Ticket Information

Starting at 8 a.m., we hand out up to two numbered tickets per person in the Main Room. Your ticket number reserves your place in line, so feel free to grab coffee or explore the Children's and Bargain Book rooms. Please return to the Main Room a few minutes before it opens to reclaim your spot. If you miss your entry time, you will forfeit your place.

We Give 100%

While some booksellers donate a portion of your purchase to help local libraries, we donate 100% of our proceeds!
