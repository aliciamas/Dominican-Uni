Goal 3: Navigate, curate and create information across the spectrum of human records.

Outcome 3a. Design resources or tools that facilitate access to information.

The Medical Student Reference Subject Guide satisfies Outcome 3a as it's a resource that facilitates access to information. This subject guide provides an introduction to the website and features reference books, each with a description, summary, evaluation, and location informationâ€”either found in the library or on the library's website.

Recognizing the abundance of resources available at the medical library, I realized important resources might easily be overlooked. To address this, I focused on core reference titles and created this guide to help students navigate and understand available resources. I also included online databases for quick access, presenting them in the same format as the reference books.
