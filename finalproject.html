My name is Alicia, a graduate student in the Library and Information Science program at Dominican University. This is my portfolio.

Author Snapshot: Beatrix Potter

Goal 5: Effectively communicate and collaborate to deliver, market, and advocate for library and information services.

Outcome 5b. Demonstrate effective professional communication to achieve common understanding as an individual or in group settings.

With the Author Snapshot presentation on Beatrix Potter, I have demonstrated Outcome 5b. which calls for a project that conveys professional communication to achieve a common understanding in a group setting. This project was created for a professional audience, and I communicated that information professionally to them. I shared this presentation with my instructor as well as with my classmates so that they can learn about Beatrix Potter as a children's book author. With this information provided to them, we were able to achieve a common understanding.

Core Collection: Spanish-Language Popular Literature

Goal 2: Understand the essential nature of information and its relevance to our diverse society.

Outcome 2c. Develop appropriate responses to assessed information needs within diverse communities/organizations.

This Core Collection satisfies Outcome 2c as it was created for Rebecca Crown Library in response to a data-supported need for Spanish-language books in the collection. As Dominican University is a Hispanic Serving Institution (HSI), with 55% of the total student body being Hispanic according to the Dominican University student profile for Fall 2023, it was surprising to find a gap in the collection here. In response to this assessed information need, I created a core collection of 10 titles originally written in Spanish by Latinx authors to offer a more diverse reading experience for our diverse community.
